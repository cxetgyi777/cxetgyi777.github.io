Mr.Robot(TV Series)-Overview

Mr.Robot is a critically acclaimed American psychological thriller and cyberpunk drama TV series that premiered in 2015. Created by Sam Esmail,the show follows Elliot Alderson(played by Rami Malek),a brilliant but troubled cybersecurity engineer and hacker who suffers from social anxiety,depression,and dissociative identity disorder.


Plot Summary

By day,Elliot works as a cybersecurity enginner at Allsafe, a company that protects major corporations. By night, he acts as a vigilante hacker, exposing criminals and corrupt individuals.His life takes a dramatic turn when he is recruited by a mysterious anarchist know as Mr.Robot (Christian slater) to join fsociety,a hacktivist group aiming to take down the world's largest conglomerate,E Corp (which Elliot refers to as "Evil Corp").

As Elliot gets deeper into fsociety's plans,he becomes entangled in a high-stakes mission to erase global debt by hacking and collapsing the financial system.However,he soon realizes that nothing is as it seems,ans he struggles with his own mental instability, unreliable perceptions,and powerful enemies.
